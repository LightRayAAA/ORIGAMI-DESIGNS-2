    • Interesting facts about cicadas!

  • Cicadas are generally pretty noisy, mainly to scare off potential predators, especially during the night, when their predators, such as owls and cicada killers are most active.
  • Some humans eat cicadas!
  • Cicada wings are immune to the effects of water and bacteria.


    • Interesting facts about pandas!

  • Some pandas are picky and will only consume one or two types of bamboo.
  • Panda cubs are usually only a couple of inches long.
  • Pandas poop about 40 times a day!


    • Interesting facts about pigeons!

  • Pigeons, like other birds are surprisingly intelligent. They use advanced techniques to retrive food, in ways that most animals cannot.
  • Pigeons can navigate very well! Because of this, they used to be used to deliver messages!
  • Pigeons can fly up to a speed of almost 90 miles per hour! (140-150 kph)


    • Interesting facts about teddy bears!
